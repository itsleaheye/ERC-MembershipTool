What is the ERC?

The Edmonton Rocketry Club (ERC) is an Edmonton club dedicated to the promotion of model rocketry as a hobby!

What Do We Do?

The ERC holds monthly meetings and endeavors. We also host regular launch sessions as our Canadian weather permits.

Our meetings are free and all ages are welcome!

Join us and meet other people interested in model rocketry. Also, feel free to join in on our group discussion about anything rocket related, find out about upcoming rocket launches, and even get help with your model rocket builds.
