Interested in flying model rockets with us? Take a look at what it takes to become an ERC member

Why join the
Edmonton Rocketry Club?

We offer our members some really cool perks! Take a look below, at some of the awesome things that come with joining the ERC.

Attend

  • Club workshops
  • Build sessions
  • Local rocket launches
  • Our high power rocketry mentorship programs

Local ERC Launches

  • Access to (1) free A or B motor
  • Access to our launch equipment
  • Members can launch up to G motors (regularly limited to D motors)

Exclusive Contacts

  • Receive club newsletters, launch dates, etc.
  • GoogleGroup access to directly interact with other members
  • Join our discussions during our monthly meetings & AGM

Reduced Launch Fees

  • Local ERC fees are waived for members (regularly $5.00)
  • Fire & Ice fees are $20 for members (regularly $25.00)

Discounts At

  • The Canadian Rocket Store - Get 5% off your orders

Membership Application and Fee

Annual

  Adult (18+): $30

  Youth (17 and under): $15

Additional Memberships

**Purchasing multiple memberships within the same household, applies the following discounts**

  Adult (18+): $20

  Youth (17 and under): $10
