Our Upcoming Events

Please keep in mind: Our monthly club meetings are normally held at the Telus World of Science. But due to current world events, we will be holding our meetings over Zoom until further notice.

November

Model Rocket Launch Is A GO!

November 5 @ 12:00-16:00

This meeting is free and all ages are welcome!

Join us and meet other people interested in model rocketry. Join in our group discussions about anything rocket related. Find out about upcoming rocket launches and other events, and even get help with your model rocket builds.
This meeting will include a "Show & Tell". We ask those that want to participate to show us your rockets over Zoom video, and share what you are building or have built.

**We will post the Zoom meeting link closer to the meeting date!**
